About Me

Okelue John Okpoli is a registered sponsored individual with the Securities & exchange commission (SEC). He is a startup strategist, incubator, business builder, ventures and investment expert. Most importantly, he also has a passion for SME development as a catalyst for economic development. John possesses extensive knowledge and experience cutting across Finance & Investment/ Supply chain & Logistics/E-commerce & Retail trade sectors with a consistent track record of successful startups, expansion and turnaround re-engineering of mid to large scale companies.

He holds a Special Executive MBA Programme (SEMP) Certificate from the Metropolitan School of Business and Management, United Kingdom, Master of Business Administration (MBA) and B.Sc (Mathematics & Economics) both from the University of Benin. John has membership of several professional bodies some of which are, Nigeria Institute of Management (Chartered), Institute of Credit Administration of Nigeria, Institute of Cost Management of Nigeria, Chartered Institute of Supply Chain, U.S.A-in view, Institute of Purchasing & Supply of Nigeria and Economics Institute. He has also undergone extensive trainings and courses as Executive management and Change management certificates from Chicago Institute of Business, U.S.A and Project management Institute, United Kingdom.

I have acquired over 17 plus years work experience and 8 years in senior & top management roles. He is currently the Pioneer Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer for Fundvine Hathaway Asset management Ltd (Member of the Fundvine Securities Group), where he led the set-up of the firm from scratch to obtaining Securities and Exchange Commission (S.E.C) license. Before this present assignment, He was the Managing Director for Wintar Group, where he had executive oversight for FMCG, Media production & Entertainment, Consulting and NGO subsidiaries respectively. Prior to that he was the Pioneer Chief Operating Officer AT& A Group, where I successfully set up the investment, retail and real estate divisions respectively. Significantly, he was instrumental to launching the gofood.com and go deliveries ecommerce platform.

Prior to this, he was the Pioneer Head Operations (COO) where he set up and built Sasware (Technology investment subsidiary of Signal Alliance). He once again successfully led multimillion naira investments in an early stage company and was responsible for group corporate strategy. He was also the Pioneer Marketing & Business development Manager for the defunct Thanet deliveries (Logistics arm of Buyam.com). Furthermore, He was Pioneer Group head, Ventures & Business management Philly & Mools, operators of Metro BRT and Metro taxi in Lagos. Once again, He played a major role in the turn around and relaunch of Metro taxi in 2012. John was at one time the Pioneer Marketing/business manager and set up NOC division for MF Engineering Ltd Lagos (Subsidiary of MF Engineering Ghana. He was also the Pioneer Head, marketing & client service at Orange cabs (A subsidiary of Cashlink leasing Plc).

He sits on the board of Fundvine Hathaway Asset Management Ltd – Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer, SMAP Solutions – Board member, Emerging Leaders Network (An Initiative of Centre for Values in Leadership) – President and African Gifted Women Health Initiative – Board member.
